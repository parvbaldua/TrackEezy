# TrackEezy User Guide

Welcome to **TrackEezy**! This guide will help you navigate the application and make the most of its features. Whether you are an administrator managing the shop or a staff member handling daily sales, this manual covers everything you need.

As an administrator, you have full control over the shop's data, inventory, and configuration.

### 1. Initial Setup
*   **Login**: Use your Google Account to sign in.
*   **Profile Setup**: Go to the **Profile** page to set your **Shop Name**, **Address**, **Phone Number**, and **GSTIN**. These details will appear on all your invoices.
*   **Google Sheet Connection**: Connect a Google Sheet to store your data. This sheet acts as your central database for inventory and sales records.

### 2. Managing Inventory
Navigate to the **Inventory** or **Home** page to manage your stock.
*   **Overview**: The Dashboard displays your **Total Stock Value**, **Total Products**, and **Low Stock Alerts**.
*   **Restocking**: Use the insights section to identify items that are running low (marked in red).
*   **Data Management**: You can add products directly via the Google Sheet or through the app's interfaces where available.

### 3. Reports & Insights
Go to the **Reports** page to analyze your business performance.
*   **Sales Trends**: View graphs showing your sales activity over time.
*   **Top Sellers**: Identify which products are performing best.
*   **Financials**: Track your total revenue and stock valuation day-by-day.

## üõí End User Guide (Staff & Billing)

For staff members, the primary focus is on processing sales quickly and accurately.

### 1. Billing & POS (Point of Sale)
Navigate to **Billing** or **Bill / Sell** from the home screen.

#### Standard Billing (Inventory Items)
1.  **Search**: Use the search bar to find products by name or SKU.
2.  **Add to Cart**: Click on a product to add it. You can adjust quantities using the `+` and `-` buttons.
    *   *Note*: The app automatically checks stock levels and warns you if you try to sell more than what is available.
3.  **Review**: Check the cart summary for Subtotal and Tax.
4.  **Checkout**:
    *   **WhatsApp**: Send the bill directly to the customer's phone.
    *   **Print**: Print a physical receipt.
    *   **Complete Sale**: Click to finalize. This **automatically deducts** the sold items from your inventory.

#### Manual Billing (Custom Sales)
Navigate to **Manual Billing** for flexible sales.
*   **Quick Picks**: Set up frequently sold items for one-tap access.
*   **Custom Entry**: Manually enter the **Price**, **Quantity**, **Discount %**, and **GST** for items not in your inventory.
*   **Invoices**: Generate professional invoices in **POS (Thermal)** or **A4** format.
*   **Share**: Download the invoice as a PDF or share it link via WhatsApp.

### 2. Checking Stock
If a customer asks about availability:
*   Use the **Search** bar on the Billing page.
*   The product card will display the current **Stock Level** (e.g., "Stock: 45 pieces").

### 3. Digital Receipts
TrackEezy supports eco-friendly digital billing.
*   **QR Code**: Every bill generates a QR code that customers can scan to view their digital receipt.
*   **WhatsApp Integration**: Sending a bill via WhatsApp is faster and saves paper.
